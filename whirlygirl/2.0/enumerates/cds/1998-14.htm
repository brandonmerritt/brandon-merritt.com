Geoff Farina ... Usonian Dream Sequence
Southern Records

Sometime in October, I was in Newbury Comics catching up on a backlog of Karate releases. The guy ringing up my purchases voiced his disappointment in Karate's The Bed Is in the Ocean, saying it was too much Geoff and not enough Karate. I said then and will say again, "there is never too much Geoff Farina." Unique chord progressions, simplistic production, sheer poetry, and flawless musicianship are just a few of his assets. Quietly dramatic and passionately reserved, Usonian Dream Sequence showcases poignant songs and songwriting. Kudos: If only my writing could be as grounded and still as interesting. Magic.
