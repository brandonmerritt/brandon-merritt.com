twenty-five favorite CDs of 1998
3 February 1999

My version of those ubiquitous year-end lists that consume our attention spans at the end of every year. My first html version of this list included a collage of each of the twenty-five artists on my list and the challenge to name them all. I think Ramona was the only one to try... and even she kept pointing at one of the guys and saying "Bob Mould"... even though he doesn't appear on my list. Hmmmm.

Now it's May 1999 when I'm throwing this new design together and I already regret some of my choices. I guess we never outgrow the embarrassment for who we are in that frozen moment of time... there in the past. We reinvent ourselves... pass through the person in that moment... our perspective changes. But like flipping through ancient snap-shots of the bodies we once inhabited, we should find some kind of peace revisiting the perspectives we once held.
