Less Than Jake ... Hello Rockview
Capitol Records

I love these guys. I really do. I couldn't have lived with myself if I'd let them slip off my list. Hello Rockview, however, is the kind of a disappointment that only a true fan with high expectations can have. There are some definite gems: "Help Save the Youth of America from Exploding", "All My Best Friends Are Metalheads", and "History of a Boring Town" are all vintage Jake as is the rerelease of a true vintage single "Scott Farcas Takes It on the Chin", but most of the other songs often served simply as reminders to program my CD player next time. Kudos: Two trombones sound better than one. Welcome, Pete!
