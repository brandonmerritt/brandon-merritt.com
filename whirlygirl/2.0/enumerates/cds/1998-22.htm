The Green Pajamas ... All Clues Lead to Meagan's Bed
Camera Obscura

It's hard to put a finger on what makes a few indie rock bands stand out from the crowd, but The Green Pajamas have that secret ingredient. Interchangeably piano and guitar driven, reflective and obnoxious, All Clues Lead to Meagan's Bed offers variety as well as a fantastic title. Songs that, more than anything else, evoke moods: the first lines of "Queen of Sunshine" are sadly intriguing while "The Secret of Her Smile" sparks fiery independence. Kudos: Grandiose refrains that sweep me away in thought.
