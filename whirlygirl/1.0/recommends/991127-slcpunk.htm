slc punk
Directed by James Merendino; with Matthew Lillard, Michael A. Goorjian, Jennifer Lien
27 November 1999

 

 

Charm.

This film gives off such an up-front, confrontational dose of charm. Matthew Lillard as Stevo speaks directly to you, sweeping you into his life, his friends, his attitude, his mantra. He suffocates you with this contagious enthusiasm. Injects you with his charm.

Watching this film is like following a writer through the process of creating characters and story. Each dive the director takes into pockets of background information and character development becomes a separate, enthralling story. Then suddenly fingers are snapped and the narrative moves forward, leaving you more entranced and committed than before. Pulled closer like a magnet.

Your unconscious investment leads to a handsome reward... a film that shades your perspective, leaves you drowning in self-reflection, and satiates your desire for something left of center.
