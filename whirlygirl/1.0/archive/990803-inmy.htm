today's temporarily skewed perspective...
When Jim called today he mentioned that his brother, Conor, had been hiking the Appalachian Trail since March and was going to finish in about two weeks. I can't figure out how this fact escaped his emails over the past five months but I quickly called up Conor's AT Journal site to catch up on twelve states of hiking. After I'd been reading them for an hour or so, I found myself thinking, "Wait? He had another zero mile day? Two late starts in a row? Is he backpacking or slackpacking?"
And then reality would come into view again quickly: I get winded walking up a few flights of stairs and I'm going to criticize Conor for taking a day or two or three off in the middle of a six month mountain hike? Am I certifiable?
