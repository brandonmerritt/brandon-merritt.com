how does it feel?
Last night Elizabeth Berg came into the store and after a brief conversation where I stuttered clumsily and she humored me by smiling, I watched her look through the fiction shelves. I couldn't imagine what it must feel like for her to go into a bookstore and stare up at her own books on the shelf.
