marie's crisis cafe
27 August 1999

 

 

The queens at Marie's go round and round. Ear-breaking choruses by rowdy, Broadway wannabes. Grove Street and Seventh Avenue, liquid with people under a nearly-full moon, all carousing after midnight. Tate calls to Brent, up ahead of him walking past Sweets Basils, eyes lit with the excitement of a dearest wish come true.
